Test FPCW is now offering telehealth services to all of our patients due to the coronavirus (COVID-19) outbreak. We are offering telemedicine, telepsychiatry and tele-counseling.

Telehealth relates to delivery of health care via video conference. We do our best to make the experience patient-centered, affordable, and effective for individuals who need psychiatric care. Due to the coronavirus outbreak, it has become an essential tool for continuity of treatment.
